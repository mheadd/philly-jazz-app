All That Philly Jazz

Philadelphia is the birthplace of our democracy. This quintessential American city made a major contribution to jazz, America's unique art form.

All That Philly Jazz is mapping Philadelphia's rich jazz legacy. We are mapping Philly's historic places, including the John Coltrane House, markers, Walk of Fame plaques and murals. We will breathe life into legendary jazz clubs like the Showboat, Pep's, Blue Note, Up Jumped the Devil, Fantasy Lounge, and clubs along 52nd Street, aka "The Strip." Clubs like the Aqua Lounge, Top Shelf, Mr. Silk's Third Base and Foo-Foo Regan's.

We will take visitors back to the days when jazz legends performed at the Uptown, the Royal, Earle Theater, and the Lincoln Theater. To contextualize the images, we will include location-based audio and information about the performers.

Help us tell the story. You can share your memories here.
